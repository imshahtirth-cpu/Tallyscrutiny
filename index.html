High Risk Ledger Analysis

18 sophisticated detection rules across 4 ledger groups — automatically surfaces loan routing, cash cycling, and undocumented transactions that require investigation.

UNSEC LOAN RULES
SUNDRY CREDITORS — HR-01 to HR-03
HR-01 CREDITOR Payment Only — no purchase/sale/journal activity. Probable cash routing through creditor.
HR-02 CREDITOR Receipt Only — money received from creditor. Highly unusual; warrants immediate investigation.
HR-03 CREDITOR Payment + Receipt Mix — zero business transactions, only cash movement. Suspicious.
SUNDRY DEBTORS — HR-04 to HR-06
HR-04 DEBTOR Receipt Only — no sales/journal activity. Probable unrecorded income.
HR-05 DEBTOR Payment Only — money paid to debtor. Highly unusual; may indicate reverse routing.
HR-06 DEBTOR Payment + Receipt Mix — no business transactions, only P&R. Red flag.
LOANS & ADVANCES (ASSET) — HR-07 to HR-09
HR-07 LOAN A/C Payment Only — disbursed without proper journal booking. May be undisclosed loan.
HR-08 LOAN A/C Receipt Only — recovered without proper entry. Unrecorded income risk.
HR-09 LOAN A/C Payment + Receipt Mix — pure cash routing through loan account. High risk.
UNSECURED / SECURED LOANS — HR-10 to HR-12
HR-10 UNSEC LOAN Receipt Only — borrowed without documentation. Section 269SS risk.
HR-11 UNSEC LOAN Payment Only — repaid without journal entry. Unexplained outflow.
HR-12 UNSEC LOAN Payment + Receipt Mix — no documentation at all. Maximum risk level.
⚠ AUTO-EXCLUSIONS: GST · TDS · TCS · VAT · CST · Income Tax · Insurance · Subscription · Prepaid · Provision · Salary · Wages · Round Off · Discount · Depreciation — automatically excluded to eliminate false positives. Use F5 to filter by specific rule code.
