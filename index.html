High Risk Ledger Analysis — 18 rules

The flagship report. Applies pattern rules across four ledger groups to surface loan routing and cash cycling that's easy to miss scrolling through vouchers by hand.

RulesLedger groupPattern flagged
HR-01–03Sundry CreditorsPayment-only, receipt-only, or payment+receipt with zero business activity
HR-04–06Sundry DebtorsReceipt-only, payment-only, or payment+receipt with zero business activity
HR-07–09Loans & Advances (Asset)Disbursed or recovered without a matching journal entry
HR-10–12Unsecured / Secured LoansBorrowed or repaid with no supporting documentation — Sec 269SS risk

Auto-excluded: GST · TDS · TCS · VAT · Income Tax · Insurance · Salary · Wages · Round Off · Discount · Depreciation — so routine ledgers don't clutter the list. Filter by rule with F5.
